Full Overview

OldGold is a modern platform for buying and selling old, unused, or scrap gold through a secure, transparent, and user-friendly workflow.

  • Valuation-to-sale transaction flow
  • Transparent pricing and reduced hidden complexity
  • Designed for old jewelry, broken ornaments, and unused gold assets
  • Trust-focused digital experience for a traditionally offline market
Domain & Audience

OldGold serves users who need a focused digital tool in its domain rather than a generic platform. The design goal is clarity, usefulness, and a low-friction path from discovery to action.

Workflow

The workflow is organized so users can understand the product purpose quickly, access the external platform, and return to the ANISH ENTERPRISES ecosystem for related work.

Engineering Focus

The implementation direction emphasizes responsive interface structure, accessible content, reliable navigation, and maintainable product positioning.
