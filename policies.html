Privacy Policy

Purpose: ANISH ENTERPRISES ("Company", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of visitors, clients, contractors, and research participants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, store, share, and protect personal data in connection with our services, website, research activities, products, and communications.

Scope: This policy covers personal data collected via our website https://AnishEnterprises.site, offline interactions, contractual engagements, security services (including penetration testing and vulnerability assessments), research programs, and enterprise products. It applies to all natural persons (visitors, users, clients, researchers) whose personal data ANISH ENTERPRISES processes.

Definitions:

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (as defined in the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023).
  • Sensitive Personal Data: Data of a sensitive nature including health, biometric, financial authentication details, or any other class identified by applicable law.
  • Controller: ANISH ENTERPRISES when deciding purposes and means of processing personal data.
  • Processor: A third party processing personal data on behalf of ANISH ENTERPRISES.
  • Data Subject: The individual whose personal data is processed.

Applicability: This policy applies to all business units, employees, contractors, service providers, and partners handling personal data on behalf of the Company.

User Responsibilities: Individuals must provide accurate personal information, exercise rights under applicable data protection laws (access, correction, erasure, data portability where applicable), follow reasonable authentication and security instructions when accessing account areas or receiving support, and, for clients commissioning security services, supply valid authorization and necessary scope details.

Company Responsibilities: ANISH ENTERPRISES will process personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently; implement privacy-by-design in services, products, and research; maintain records of processing activities; provide mechanisms for data subject requests; and apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure data.

Security Considerations: We implement access controls, encryption at-rest and in-transit for personal data where feasible, role-based access, logging and monitoring, secure SDLC practices, and vulnerability management; retain data per documented schedules and securely dispose of it when appropriate; and use pseudonymization and minimization where full identification is unnecessary.

Compliance Requirements: ANISH ENTERPRISES is guided by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and the Information Technology Act, 2000. Cross-border transfers will follow the DPDP Act rules; we perform lawful transfer mechanisms and risk assessments before international transfers.

Legal Basis: Processing is performed based on contractual necessity, legitimate interests (weighing data subject rights), consent (where required), legal obligation, or other lawful bases under applicable law. Security testing and incident response processing are also necessitated by obligations to protect clients and the ecosystem.

Exceptions: Aggregated, anonymized data used for analytics and research if suitably de-identified; legal holds and compelled disclosures where required by authorities.

Enforcement: Violations by employees may lead to disciplinary action up to termination; contractual remedies apply for third-party processor breaches.

Reporting Procedures: Data Subject Requests: contact privacy@AnishEnterprises.site or use the web form. Report suspected data breaches to security@AnishEnterprises.site. Internal incident response will follow the Incident Response Policy and notify CERT-In and affected data subjects per DPDP Act timelines where required.

Best Practices: Limit collection to necessary fields; enable encryption; maintain retention and deletion schedules; perform privacy impact assessments (PIAs); and vet suppliers.

FAQ: How do I request access or deletion? Submit a verified request to privacy@AnishEnterprises.site. We will verify identity and respond within statutory timelines.

Cross References: Data Protection Policy; Information Security Policy; Incident Response Policy; Responsible Vulnerability Disclosure Policy; Cookie Policy; Analytics Policy.

Cybersecurity Policy

Purpose: This policy sets ANISH ENTERPRISES’ organizational cybersecurity governance, roles, responsibilities, standards for secure operations, and expectations for internal teams, contractors, and clients.

Scope: Internal IT systems, cloud services, client-engaged environments under contract, product development, and research infrastructure.

Principal Elements: NIST-aligned control objectives (Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover); vulnerability management and patching cadence; asset inventory; SAST/DAST; OWASP Top 10 mitigations; CVSS-based risk classification and SLAs; incident detection and escalation; and adherence to CERT-In advisories. The policy mandates written Rules of Engagement and signed authorization for third-party testing, safe-harbor principles for good-faith reporting, and secure evidence collection procedures for forensic readiness.

Reporting: Report security issues to security@AnishEnterprises.site. Critical incidents are escalated to Incident Response and CERT-In when appropriate.

Responsible Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

Purpose: To provide a coordinated process for receiving, acknowledging, triaging, and remediating vulnerability reports from external researchers and internal stakeholders.

Key Procedures: Acknowledge receipt within 72 hours; triage using CVSS; provide remediation timelines and updates; offer safe harbor for good-faith reporters that follow this policy; and escalate qualifying incidents to Incident Response and CERT-In as required. Reports should be sent to security@AnishEnterprises.site with the subject line "Vulnerability Report: [Product/System]". Use PGP for sensitive submissions (PGP key available on request).
